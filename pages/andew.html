EX03

The GCSEs are a series of exams in every subject, and students taking them are required to pass them to attend University. Failing leads instead to alternate occupations, as far as I can gather, akin to the trades. These exams are so centralizing that multiple years of education are devoted entirely to preparing for them. This is detrimental to the education of the students, as by educating to these exams students are given a transactional view of education where the purpose of learning is to prepare for an exam. This is harmed further by the amount of time focused on preparing students for this one specific exam. If these classes are taught anything like AP classes here, then students will learn techniques that, while not good for use in a professional setting, earn points on these exams. This is especially harmful to the humanities, where work is more expressive, and a formula is especially crippling.


EX04

Aforza is a platform designed to help companies increase profits via increasing their efficiency. They have different products targeted toward different areas, claiming to help a company to "Plan, Visit, Sell and Deliver in real time." They have tools to automatically and quickly identify areas of improvement to allow for optimization to address the needs of a company. Data collection and management to better market products play key roles: planning more optimized routes, inventory and demand management, and price analysis. This data analysis also helps them manage consistency, which allows a company to build trust. They also have a list of partners on their website who help with product design, ensuring a timely and quality delivery. Their self-marketing is very results based, focusing on a list of companies that have benefitted from employing Alforza's technology and solutions to increase visibility and profit margins efficiently. To this end, there's both a full page of clients, as well as an abbreviated list thereof underneath every page. On their website, they have links to demo their products to allow someone to test how well a fit Alforza would be. They also include pipelines allowing to look at their current partners, including which web services they use, as well as ways to apply to partner with Aforza.
